                  Jeremy Zhang
                    Cofounder,
                      Finch
                    San Francisco, California
              From the Editor
            Jeremy Zhang cofounded Finch in 2020 to build tools that connect payroll, HR and benefits systems together. The startup has raised $19 million from investors including Menlo Ventures and Y Combinator and serves hundreds of customers including Carta and Betterment. Prior to starting Finch, Zhang worked on robotics at Amazon and helped build Smartcar's software for automobiles.
