Biography

PRIOR TO CORNELL
Two-time D3 John Wooden Player of the Year (2022, 2023) ...  D2 All-CIF Southern Section Player of the Year (2023) ... D3 All-CIF Southern Section Player of the Year (2022) ... D1 All-Southern Section second team (2024) ... two-time first-team All-League Del Rey (2023, 2024) ... first-team All-League Camino Real (2022) ... D3 2022 CIF California State Runner-ups ... D3 2022 CIF California Southern Regionals Champions ... D3 2022 CIF California Southern Sectional Champions ... D2 2023 CIF California Southern Sectional Champions ... also played volleyball her sophomore year ... member of the Mu Alpha Theta Honors Society, the Science National Honors Society, the National Honors Society, and the Spanish Honors Society ... also was selected to be a part of the Senior Senate, a student body advisory group that is teacher and principal appointed.

PERSONAL
Audrey Lana Chen is the daughter of Charles and Lisa Chen and has one sister ... her sister, Ellie Chen, is a guard at San Diego State University ... she is enrolled in the College of Engineering.
