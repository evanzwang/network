Replo helps serious Shopify teams sell more on the Internet.

Our platform helps teams craft, measure, and test winning campaigns.

Replo is a cutting-edge platform designed to revolutionize customer acquisition for Shopify businesses. Our mission is to make customer acquisition faster, more efficient, and more effective for ecommerce teams.

Founded in 2021 by Yuxin Zhu and Noah Gilmore, we've experienced rapid growth, surpassing 1,000 paying customers in just six months with no sales or marketing budget. To date, we’ve raised over $8.2M from Figma, YCombinator, Vercel, General Catalyst, Infinity Ventures, MAGIC Fund, Base Case Capital. We're headquartered in San Francisco.

JOIN OUR TEAM

Our Mission

Replo is dedicated to empowering teams to craft, measure, and test winning campaigns. Our mission is to make customer acquisition faster, more efficient, and more effective for ecommerce businesses at all stages of growth.

Our Expertise

Replo has a deep understanding of the Shopify ecosystem and the challenges that businesses face in acquiring new customers. We have extensive experience in marketing, sales, and technology, and we are committed to providing our customers with the best possible solutions.

Our Values

Innovation and collaboration are at the core of everything we do. We are committed to providing exceptional customer service and maintaining the highest standards of integrity in all our interactions.
