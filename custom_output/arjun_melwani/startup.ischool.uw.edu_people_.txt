              Entrepreneurs-in-residence (EIRs)
              Our EIRs support our community events and provide mentorship to first-time student founders. EIRs are typically UW students who bring substantial prior experience as entrepreneurs or VC investors. If you’re an iSchool student and would like to schedule a mentoring session with an EIR, please
                [contact us](https://startup.ischool.uw.edu/contact/)
