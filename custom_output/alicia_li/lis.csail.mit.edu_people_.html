Meet the Team

The people behind the LIS Group.

Members of LIS work in a broad set of research areas. Our research brings together ideas from motion and task planning, machine learning, reinforcement learning, and computer vision to synthesize robot systems that are capable of behaving intelligently across a wide range of problem domains.
