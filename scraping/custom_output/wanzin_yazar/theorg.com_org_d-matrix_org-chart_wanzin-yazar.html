Wanzin Yazar

Staff ML Research Engineer at d-Matrix

Wanzin Yazar is a Staff ML Research Engineer with experience in developing productionized machine learning models from large healthcare datasets. Wanzin has worked at companies like d-Matrix, Nephrosant, Inc., and Advancers.ai. Wanzin's expertise includes achieving better neural network architecture on an automated malaria classification system and publishing a technical paper on upgrading AI-Powered mobile applications. Wanzin also worked as a Machine Learning Researcher at UC Berkeley Division of Computing, Data Science, and Society, focusing on deep learning-based recommender systems and student enrollment predictions using clustering practices and regression models. Wanzin holds a Bachelor's degree in Applied Mathematics (AI) from the University of California, Berkeley.
