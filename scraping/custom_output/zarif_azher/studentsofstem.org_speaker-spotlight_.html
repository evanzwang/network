Speaker Spotlight

In partnership with Discourse—a YouTube channel started by our content director Vatsal Sivaratri—Students of STEM is spotlighting speakers with computer science experience to give an inside perspective to the ever-changing field. For a complete playlist of guest talks, check out Discourse here.

Ep. 5 Part I: Zarif Azher on Bioinformatics, BioCode, and Omics-Data Prognostics

Zarif Azher is a bioinformatics researcher who strives to bridge the gap between science and policy making and making healthcare more accessible. We talk about his work in multimodal learning, and his work in science fairs and labs.

ㅤ

Ep. 4: Dr. John Leddo on AI Educational Software, Anti-Aging, and AI in Education

Dr. John Leddo is an educational psychologist with over 30 years of experience. Recently, he’s incorporated AI as a means to better educate his students, as well as pursue some of his own passion projects.

ㅤ

Ep 3: Aryaman Khanna on TJML, Autonomous Vehicles, and Blockchain Technology

Aryaman Khanna, an accomplished student ML researcher, shares insights into his work on self-driving cars and his project on a decentralized blockchain network for secure data exchange between vehicles. He explores the fascinating world of vehicle automation and blockchain technology.

ㅤ

Ep. 2: Dr. Nisha Talagala on her journey in AI, AI Literacy, and AIClub & AIClub Pro

Dr. Nisha Talagala, a leader in Machine Learning and AI literacy, discusses her journey in AI and her passion. We also talk about her education AI organizations, AIClub, and AIClub Pro, and the motivation behind these.

ㅤ

Ep. 1: Sauman Das on Club Leadership, Healthcare Inequity, and AI in Medicine

Sauman Das, a freshman at UPenn M&T, shares his journey in club leadership, his mission to address healthcare disparities through technology, and healthcare inequities. We talk more about leadership, and the future of AI in healthcare.

ㅤ

Ep. 0: Introducing Discourse – Exploring the World of AI Together!

Join Vatsal Sivaratri, a high school junior, on a journey through the world of Artificial Intelligence (AI).

ㅤ
