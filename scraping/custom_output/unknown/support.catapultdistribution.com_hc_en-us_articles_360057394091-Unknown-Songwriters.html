Unknown Songwriters

While you should make every effort to submit and properly credit the full name of the actual Songwriter for every track that you submit, we realize this is not always possible.*

If the unknown Songwriter for a given track is for a very old, archaic or traditional song (ie: Historical chants, traditional folk songs, etc.) when you submit the Songwriter/Composer name you can click on "I don't know the songwriter / composer" as shown below:

 

You NEVER submit a songwriter name as "Public Domain, "DAR", etc. as this makes literally no sense. 

*The majority of your album's tracks must have a known songwriter. We will not distribute an album if the majority of the songwriters are unknown by you.
