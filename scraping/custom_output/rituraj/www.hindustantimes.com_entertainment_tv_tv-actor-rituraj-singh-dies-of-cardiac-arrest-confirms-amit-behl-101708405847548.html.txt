          Rituraj Singh's sudden death after cardiac arrest shocks entertainment industry
                By
                  [HT Entertainment Desk](/author/ht-entertainment-desk-101608310311943)
            Feb 20, 2024 06:57 PM IST
                    Share Via
                    Copy Link
              socialEl
          Rituraj Singh, known for his role of Yashpal in hit serial Anupamaa, died on Monday night at the age of 59 after a cardiac arrest.
                Television actor Rituraj Singh has died after a cardiac arrest. He was 59. As per
                  [Times Now News](https://www.timesnownews.com/entertainment-news/tv/anupamaa-fame-rituraj-singh-passes-away-at-59-close-friend-amit-behl-confirms-article-107840105)
                  , his close friend and actor Amit Behl has confirmed his death.
                  Rituraj Singh has died at 59.
              As per the report, Rituraj died on Monday night. He was recently hospitalised as he was suffering from a pancreatic disease.
              (Also read:
                [Hansal Mehta, Vivek Agnihotri, Sonu Sood condole death of Anupamaa actor Rituraj Singh: ‘Untapped actor and warm human’](https://www.hindustantimes.com/entertainment/tv/hansal-mehta-vivek-agnihotri-sonu-sood-condole-death-of-anupamaa-actor-rituraj-singh-101708407890222.html)
                )
              “He had been hospitalised due to some stomach issues and was discharged a few days ago. He passed away around 12.30 am at his home due to a cardiac arrest,” Amit Behl told PTI.
              Taking to X (formerly Twitter), actor Arshad Warsi wrote, "I am so saddened to know that Ritu Raj passed away. We lived in the same building, he was a part of my first film as a producer. Lost a friend and a great actor… will miss you brother…"
                [Hansal Mehta](https://www.hindustantimes.com/topic/hansal-mehta)
                wrote, "Rituraj!!!! Can’t believe this! I directed him briefly in a daily soap called K Street Pali Hill but in the process we became good friends. Been a while since we hung out but I have such fond memories. Untapped actor and a warm human. Gone suddenly and too early."
              Vivek Agnihotri also posted a photo and wrote, "Rituraj, my friend, how did you make it even possible? 'Kitna baaki tha… (so much was left).' Artists never die. Om Shanti."
              Rituraj's many roles
              Rituraj has starred in many shows like Banegi Apni Baat, Jyoti, Hitler Didi, Shapath, Warrior High, Aahat, Adaalat, Diya and Aur Baati Hum. He also played the role of Balwant Choudhary in the TV serial Laado 2.
              The actor has also featured in films such as Badrinath Ki Dulhania (2017), Vash- Possessed by the Obsessed and Thunivu (2023). His last film was Yaariyan 2 which released last year.
              Rituraj was also part of several web series, including The Test Case, Hey Prabhu, Criminal, Abhay, Bandish Bandits, Never Kiss Your Best Friend and Made in Heaven season 2. Before his death, he played the role of Yashpal in the serial Anupamaa.
