    Biography
    The moment you step into his office, you can tell fellowship-trained geriatrician, Alvin Chang, MD, cares about seniors. "I enjoy taking care of older adults and listening to their stories. I learn so much from them. I see myself as an advocate, so I really pay attention to their concerns. I try to get to know who they are." To help those with limited mobility, Dr. Chang makes house calls to nursing homes and assisted living facilities. Dr. Chang enjoys spending time with his family.
