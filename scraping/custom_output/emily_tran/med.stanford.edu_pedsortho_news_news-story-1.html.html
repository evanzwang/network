Stanford Student Research

Each year, students from Stanford University, School of Medicine assist our team in developing research projects and questions. Students are paired with a member of our faculty based on their research interests. Many of our students get the opportunity to present their research at national conferences. If you are interested in learning more about our student research program, please email Amishi Jobanputra, the clinical research manager, at amishij@stanford.edu.
