Hi! I’m George.

Researcher | Founder | Public Speaker

George Cheng is a researcher, environmentalist, and innovator who uses STEM for Humanity to uplift vulnerable communities.

Through his research at Westlake University, North Carolina State University, University of North Carolina - Chapel Hill, and Harvard Medical School, he has published 5 papers and worked on several projects ranging from A.I. avatars to wearable medical devices.

A World Science Scholar, he has been recognized by Regeneron International Science and Engineering Fair (ISEF), National Junior Science and Humanities Symposium (JSHS), United States Stockholm Junior Water Prize, Coca-Cola, Department of Defense, and International BioGENEius Institute.

An athlete, George is a Heisman High School Scholarship National Winner and plays 4 varsity sports.

As an Eagle Scout, George is currently tackling socioeconomic issues through his nonprofit organizations and has raised $150,000 for homeless artists, cancer patients, and Title I schools.

An Earth Prize Winner and recipient of the Explorers Grant, George is also tackling climate change and water accessibility through collaborations with Water Environment Federation (WEF), TacoBell Foundation, and Wolfram.

George plans to pursue Computer Science (AI + Decision Making), Mathematics, and Finance at MIT.
