    Overview

    Dr. Debajyoti Datta is a hematologist in San Francisco, California and is affiliated with UCSF Health-UCSF Medical Center. He received his medical degree from University of California (San Francisco) School of Medicine and has been in practice between 11-20 years. Dr. Debajyoti Datta accepts Medicare, Blue Cross - see other insurance plans accepted.
