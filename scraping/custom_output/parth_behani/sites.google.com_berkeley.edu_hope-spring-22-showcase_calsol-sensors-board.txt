                                  Sean Yang, Michael Jia, Parth Behani
                                    We've designed a board to take inputs from multiple sensors over and communicate them with a main vehicle computer via CAN. The data transmitted will be used by CalSol's 10th generation solar power vehicle, Excalibur, to make strategy decisions at race time. Onboard are sensors for humidity, temperature, pressure, an accelerometer, a thermopile sensor and a thermistor.
                                  MCU and Peripherals
                                  CAN interface (Left), Voltage Regulators (Right)
                                  Passives soldered by reflow
                                  ICs / Connectors hand soldered
